About

Advanced Download Manager v1.0.0

A Chrome extension that intercepts downloads and allows you to route them to various download managers with automatic cookie forwarding.

Author

derek
GitHub: https://github.com/hereisderek

Features

  • Intercept all downloads before they start
  • Route to Chrome's built-in downloader
  • Forward to local download managers (FDM, IDM, cURL, wget)
  • Send to remote downloaders (qBittorrent, aria2)
  • Automatic cookie extraction and forwarding
  • Referrer header support

Usage

  1. Configure your preferred downloaders in the settings
  2. When you start a download, a popup will appear
  3. Choose your preferred download method
  4. The download will be forwarded with all necessary cookies

Privacy

This extension only accesses cookies for the specific URLs you download. Cookies are never sent to any third-party services except the downloaders you explicitly configure.
